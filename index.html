About Me

Hello, my name is Debra and welcome to my website! I moved from Los Angeles, California to Atlanta, Georgia in 2016. After coming from a background of working extensively in Digital Marketing, e-Commerce, and Analytics, I decided to dive head first into programming. Now, I'm coding every day as a full stack developer studying at DigitalCrafts, an immersive 16-week web development bootcamp focused on Javascript and Python. I am always learning new things and seeking new challenges along the way, and I hope that is reflected in the progress of my work as I aspire to become a software developer. Please feel free to check out my projects and tell me what you think!
